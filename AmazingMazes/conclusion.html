Conclusion

    The exhibitor's hypothesis was wrong. Goaly (the mouse that will always go in the open direction that is nearest to the end of the maze) was the winner. All the other strategies were worse.



    Project Variations

    Several things could change the results of this project.

    If the exhibitor had more time he could try a more complex type of maze (other than a "perfect" maze), or a maze that has cells with more than four walls.

    The mice used have total recall (they remember every place they have been). If the exhibitor had more time he could build mice that would only remember so many past steps.
