Purpose & Hypothesis



    Purpose

    What strategy can a computer-simulated mouse use to solve a maze the fastest (using the fewest number of moves)?



    Hypothesis

    I think that a computer-simulated mouse that uses a random pattern will (on average) be able to solve the maze in the fewest numbers of moves.
