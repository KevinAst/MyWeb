Old Testament

The Old Testament includes 39 books which were written long before Jesus was born.

The Old Testament books are divided into five groups:

  1. Pentateuch (Law of Moses)
  2. Historical writings
  3. Wisdom and poetry
  4. Major prophets
  5. Minor prophets
