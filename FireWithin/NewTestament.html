New Testament

New Testament

The New Testament includes 27 books about Jesus’ ministry and what it means to follow him.

The New Testament books fall into five general categories (find out more):

  1. The Gospels:
    • Matthew*
    • Mark
    • Luke
    • John
  2. The single book of Acts
    • Acts
  3. Paul’s letters to churches:
    • Romans
    • 1 Corinthians
    • 2 Corinthians
    • Galatians
    • Ephesians*
    • Philippians*
    • Colossians*
    • 1 Thessalonians
    • 2 Thessalonians
  4. Paul’s letters to church leaders:
    • 1 Timothy*
    • 2 Timothy*
    • Titus*
    • Philemon*
  5. A collection of letters sent out (mostly) to large groups of people:
    • Hebrews*
    • James*
    • 1 Peter*
    • 2 Peter*
    • 1 John*
    • 2 John*
    • 3 John*
    • Jude*
    • Revelation

Please note that this Bible Study Guide is not yet complete (asteric indicates ToDo). To request a specific book to be added, please see the Work In Progress section.
