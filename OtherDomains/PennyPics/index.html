Penny Pics
Customized Birthday/Anniversary Pictures with Pennies

Looking for that one-of-a-kind birthday/anniversary gift?


PennyPics is a unique birthday/anniversary gift idea that shows how much you really care. A PennyPic is a handsomely framed collection of pennies; one penny minted from each year of your loved one's life; with a custom message to your liking. Click on any of the pictures on this page to see the samples in more detail.


PennyPics celebrates your loved one with a customized message that personalizes how you really feel. Want to make your loved one feel good? -- "Your not getting older, your getting more valuable!"
Want to be funny? -- "Another year, another penny ... it's better than the other option!"


A Custom greeting is available on the back. Depending on the age of your loved one, there may be limited space on the front mount. At your option, you can compose a personal greeting that is displayed on the back of the mount.


PennyPics is a perfect gift idea for those special birthdays/anniversary!
